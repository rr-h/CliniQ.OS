Artist Lab

Led by an experienced team of music industry professionals, HIFI Labs provides modern A&R support to a roster of emerging artists. The A&R team at HIFI Labs focuses on artist development through web2 & web3 tools, empowering their roster of artists to take ownership of their art and creative endeavors by way of decentralized engagement and community solutions.

Idea Lab

The Idea Lab is here to bring the most original ideas to life with the greatest artists in the world.  Whether it’s an artist’s original concept or an idea we help to build with an artist’s unique talents and goals in mind, we provide the resources to pull off the most unique and creative ideas, which traditionally fell through the cracks due to lack of funding, bandwidth, and know-how.

We Won a Webby!

We won a Webby for “SURV1VAL” a music promotion/marketing campaign built for Mike Shinoda in partnership with Warner Records!

The campaign combines web2, web3 theory, and AI technology in order to drive revenue, data, and retargeting through an online game while also aimed to enhance interaction within the Shinoda community and increase music consumption across various streaming platforms.
